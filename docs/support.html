‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions can be cancelled through your device settings. On iOS, go to Settings ‚Üí Apple ID ‚Üí Subscriptions. On Android, go to Google Play Store ‚Üí Menu ‚Üí Subscriptions.

How do I export my resume?

Open your resume in the app and tap the "Export" or "Share" button. You can save it as a PDF or share it directly to other apps.

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Yes! Sign in with the same Google account on all your devices, and your resumes will sync automatically.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to the app settings and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard encryption and secure cloud storage. Your resume data is protected and only accessible by you. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
